Chemical Properties of Isopropyl Alcohol

Chemical Properties

Isopropyl alcohol is an organic compound that has various uses in daily life. This compound is the simplest secondary alcohol. As an organic compound, isopropyl alcohol has physical and chemical properties. These the chemical properties of isopropyl alcohol:

  • 1. Molar Mass : 60.1 g mol-1
  • 2. Density : 0.786 g/cm3 (at 20 degrees Celsius)
  • 3. Soluble in water.
  • 4. Soluble in benzene, chloroform, ethanol, ether, and glycerin.
  • 5. Insoluble in salt solution.
